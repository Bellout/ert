GEN_KW
------

1. Opprette en ny: ensemble_config_add_gen_kw( ensemble_config , new_key );

   Denne funksjonen oppretter en ny gen_kw_config instans og setter
   den inn i ensemble_config systemet; returverdien fra funksjonen er
   (en peker til) den nyopprettede enkf_config_node instansen.

2. Get:

      gen_kw_config = enkf_config_node_get_ref( config_node )

      gen_kw_config_get_template_file( gen_kw_config )
      gen_kw_config_get_parameter_file( gen_kw_config )
      gen_kw_config_get_init_file_fmt( gen_kw_config )
      ...................
      enkf_config_node_get_enkf_outfile( config_node )
      enkf_config_node_get_min_std_file( config_node )

3. Update:

      enkf_config_node_update_gen_kw( config_node , enkf_outfile_fmt , template_file , parameter_file , min_std_file , init_file_fmt )      
      



FIELD
-----

1. Opprette en ny: ensemble_config_add_field( ensemble_config , new_key , grid );

   Funksjonen er helt tilsvarende som for gen_kw. En grid referanse
   får du fra ecl_config_get_grid(). 

2. Get:

      field_config = enkf_config_node_get_ref( config_node )  
      
      field_config_get_truncation_mode( field_config )       - returnerer en int som er en or kombinasjon fra enumen : truncation_type.
      field_config_get_truncation_min( field_config )        - returnerer trunkerings minimum.
      field_config_get_truncation_max( field_config )        - returnerer trunkerings maximum
      field_config_get_type( field_config )                  - returnerer en field_type_enum verdi.
      field_config_get_init_transform_name( field_config )
      field_config_get_output_transform_name( field_config )
      field_config_get_init_file_fmt( field_config )  -> init files

      
      ...................
      enkf_config_node_get_enkf_outfile( config_node )  -> Include file (parameter/general)
      enkf_config_node_get_min_std_file( config_node )  -> Min. std.   (parameter/general)
      enkf_config_node_get_enkf_infile( config_node )   -> File loaded by EnKF (general)

                                               
3. Update:

    enkf_config_node_update_state_field(config_node , truncation , value_min , value_max)       
    enkf_config_node_update_parameter_field( config_node , enkf_outfile , init_files , min_std_file , truncation , min_value , max_value , init_transform , output_transform)
    enkf_config_node_update_general_field( config_node , enkf_outfile , enkf_infile , init_files , min_std_file , truncation , min_value , max_value , init_transform , 
                                           input_transform /* Denne er det ikke støtte for i GUI - du kan bare hardkode NULL*/ , output_transform)
